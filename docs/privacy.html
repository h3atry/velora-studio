Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

Velora Studio ("Velora") is a desktop application for live streaming. This policy describes how we handle information when you use the app.

Information we process

  • Account data you connect (TikTok, Twitch usernames and OAuth tokens stored locally on your device).
  • Stream settings, scenes, and chat preferences stored locally.
  • Chat messages received from connected platforms while you use the app.

Local storage

Data is stored on your computer under your Windows user profile (e.g. AppData). We do not operate a central server that collects your personal streams or keys.

Third parties

When you connect TikTok or Twitch, those platforms process data under their own policies. OAuth login is handled directly with each provider.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the Velora Studio developer through the project repository.
